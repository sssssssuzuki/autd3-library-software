29 /**
30  * @brief PointSequence provides a function to display the focus sequentially and periodically.
31  * @details PointSequence uses a timer on the FPGA to ensure that the focus is precisely timed.
32  * PointSequence currently has the following four limitations.
33  * 1. The maximum number of control points is 40000.
34  * 2. the sampling interval of Control Points is an integer multiple of 25us.
35  * 3. (number of Control Points) x (sampling interval) must be less than or equal 1 second.
36  * 4. Only a single focus can be displayed at a certain moment.
37  */
