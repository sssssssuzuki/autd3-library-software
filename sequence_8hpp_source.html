21  * @brief PointSequence provides a function to display the focus sequentially and periodically.
22  * @details PointSequence uses a timer on the FPGA to ensure that the focus is precisely timed.
23  * PointSequence currently has the following four limitations.
24  * 1. The maximum number of control points is 2000.
25  * 2. the sampling interval of Control Points is an integer multiple of 25us.
26  * 3. (number of Control Points) x (sampling interval) must be less than or equal 1 second.

28  */
